# Why SoulPrint Exists

SoulPrint exists because continuity matters.

Your AI conversations hold real thinking — decisions you made, ideas you tested, threads you never finished. That history is scattered across three platforms that forget you the moment you close a tab.

I struggle with memory too, so this is personal. I built SoulPrint because I needed it. Not as a product pitch. As a tool against fragmentation.

Nobody should have to start from zero every time they open a new chat. Nobody should lose a year of thinking because an export format changed. Your memory belongs to you — not to the platform that hosted the conversation.

SoulPrint brings your conversations home. It keeps them honest, traceable, and under your control. It does not need to impress anyone to be worth building. It just needs to work, and it does.
