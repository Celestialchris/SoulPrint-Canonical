What SoulPrint is not

Built different, on purpose

SoulPrint makes deliberate tradeoffs. Here's what sets it apart.

Not a cloud service
Your data stays on your machine
SoulPrint runs locally with SQLite. No server, no account, no data leaving your disk. You own every byte.
Not another AI wrapper
A memory system, not a chat interface
SoulPrint doesn't compete with your AI tools — it preserves the conversations you already had with them.
Not a semantic database
Canonical records with provenance
Every conversation keeps its original source, timestamp, and identity. Derived outputs are always labeled as such.
Not a dashboard
A calm, readable workspace
No metrics theater. No analytics clutter. Just your conversations, organized and searchable.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Focused tools for preserving and understanding your AI history.

Multi-provider import
Import from ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini with duplicate detection and stable IDs.
Conversation explorer
Full-context reading with table of contents, threading, and minimap navigation.
Topics and summaries
Auto-generated topics, conversation summaries, and weekly digests — labeled as derived.
Federated search
Search across all providers and lanes simultaneously with source-attributed results.
Grounded answering
Ask questions about your history and get answers with citations back to exact messages.
Memory Passport
Export a validated, portable archive of your full AI memory with provenance intact.

Trust model

Your memory, your rules

Local-first, always
SQLite on your disk. No server, no sync, no cloud dependency. Your data never leaves your machine.
Canonical provenance
Every record traces back to its original source with stable IDs. You always know where something came from.
Derived is labeled
Summaries, topics, and answers are always marked as derived — never mixed with your original conversations.
Open source
Apache-2.0 licensed. Read the code, audit the logic, fork it if you want. No black boxes.
